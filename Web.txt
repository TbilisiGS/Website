Website goal

The website should look like a modern, premium, local digital agency site made to convince small Tbilisi business owners that you can get them more customers.

It should not feel like an art portfolio.
It should feel like:

trustworthy

sharp

visually strong

simple to understand

easy to contact

The whole website should be built around one idea:

"We help Tbilili businesses get more inquiries through websites, social media, and better online presentation."

Overall style
Visual direction

Use a dark modern premium style with clean spacing.

Best look:

dark charcoal or deep navy background

white or light gray text

one strong accent color like electric blue, purple, or cyan

smooth hover animations

rounded cards

subtle shadows

clean icons

modern sans-serif font

It should look more like a premium creative growth studio than a cheap freelancer page.

Tone of design

minimal

high-end

masculine

clean

confident

not crowded

not playful

not corporate-boring

UX structure

The website should be very simple and conversion-focused.

Main rule:
A visitor should understand in 5 seconds:

what you do

who you do it for

why you are useful

how to contact you

Do not make users think too much.
