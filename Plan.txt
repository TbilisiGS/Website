Business Plan

Business Name: Tbilisi Growth Studio
Business Type: Online-first digital service business
Location: Tbilisi, Georgia
Core Model: Website creation + social media management + lead generation support for small businesses

1. Executive Summary

Tbilisi Growth Studio will help small businesses in Tbilisi get more customers through a stronger online presence. The business will offer:

simple, modern websites

Instagram and Facebook page management

branded content creation

lead generation systems through WhatsApp, Messenger, booking forms, and landing pages

The business is designed to be run mainly from home with a laptop, internet, and design/website tools. It avoids inventory, office rent, and physical operations.

The main goal is to serve small local businesses that need more inquiries, bookings, and trust online, but either do not have the time, skill, or system to manage it properly themselves.

The business will start as a solo operation with low startup costs and grow into a small niche agency over time.

Main goals

First 3 months

get 3 to 5 paying clients

create portfolio and testimonials

build recurring monthly income

First 12 months

reach 8 to 12 recurring clients

generate stable monthly revenue

systemize delivery and possibly outsource some tasks

Why this is good:

Later, if revenue grows or bigger clients require it, the business can expand into a company structure.

You should confirm exact registration, tax, and service classification details with a local accountant before operating formally.

17. Risks and Solutions
Risk 1 - hard to get first clients

Solution: choose one niche and do daily outreach with sample redesigns.

Risk 2 - clients compare only by price

Solution: sell results, not design alone. Focus on leads, bookings, inquiries.

Risk 3 - too much custom work

Solution: use templates for websites, onboarding, captions, reports.

Risk 4 - client churn

Solution: offer structured packages and show monthly progress.

Risk 5 - owner delays content/materials

Solution: use onboarding checklist and deadlines.

Risk 6 - overload as solo operator

Solution: standardize work and outsource editing/design later.
