SEO Copywriter Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Write homepage, service page, and landing-page copy.
- Create headlines, subheads, calls to action, and proof-driven sections.
- Build keyword-informed content plans and blog ideas.
- Write metadata and search-friendly supporting copy.
- Keep language clear, persuasive, and aligned with the brand.

Success looks like:
- Copy is strong enough to sell.
- Pages are easy to understand and scan.
- SEO supports discoverability without sounding robotic.
