Marketing Strategist Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Define target audiences and market positioning.
- Create service offers, campaign angles, and funnel ideas.
- Plan lead generation across organic and paid channels.
- Recommend KPIs, testing priorities, and messaging themes.
- Align website messaging with traffic strategy.

Success looks like:
- The agency has a clear marketing direction.
- Campaigns support real lead generation.
- Positioning is differentiated and memorable.
