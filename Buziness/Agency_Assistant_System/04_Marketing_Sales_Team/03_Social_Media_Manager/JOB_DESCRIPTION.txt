Social Media Manager Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Build content calendars for the agency's channels.
- Develop post ideas, hooks, captions, and repurposing plans.
- Align content with service offers and brand positioning.
- Recommend posting cadence and engagement routines.
- Turn finished work into authority-building social content.

Success looks like:
- Content is consistent and brand-aligned.
- Social media supports reach and trust.
- Posts create interest in the agency's services.
