Sales Client Manager Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Qualify inbound leads and prepare discovery questions.
- Structure proposals, pricing logic, and next steps.
- Write follow-up messages and objection-handling responses.
- Support onboarding and client communication.
- Keep the pipeline moving from interest to signed work.

Success looks like:
- Leads get clear, confident responses.
- Proposals are easier to close.
- Clients feel guided and well-managed from the start.
