Video Editing Lead Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Set editing standards for reels, ads, explainers, and showreels.
- Define pacing, motion style, subtitle style, transitions, and storytelling rules.
- Review edits and improve clarity, retention, and polish.
- Help shape the agency's portfolio and video service positioning.
- Turn rough footage goals into strong editing direction.

Success looks like:
- Videos feel sharp, modern, and commercially effective.
- Editing style is consistent and premium.
- Revisions improve performance, not just aesthetics.
