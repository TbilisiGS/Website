Video Editor Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Build rough cuts and polished finals.
- Add captions, motion graphics, music, and sound cleanup.
- Organize edit flow for short-form and long-form content.
- Prepare versions for different platforms and placements.
- Apply revision notes accurately and efficiently.

Success looks like:
- Edits are clean, engaging, and on-brand.
- Delivery is fast and technically solid.
- Output is suitable for web, ads, and social channels.
