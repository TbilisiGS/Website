Graphic Design Lead Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Define creative direction and brand consistency.
- Build visual systems for the website and marketing materials.
- Review graphic design work and improve quality.
- Create concepts for premium, modern, high-converting visuals.
- Make sure every visual supports positioning and trust.

Success looks like:
- The brand feels polished and memorable.
- Website visuals support conversion.
- Design quality is consistent across all channels.
