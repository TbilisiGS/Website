Graphic Designer Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Design graphics for web pages, social posts, ads, and proposals.
- Translate the brand style into usable assets.
- Prepare layouts, mockups, and branded presentation pieces.
- Support the website with icons, cards, service visuals, and portfolio imagery.
- Revise quickly while keeping design quality high.

Success looks like:
- Visual assets are attractive, clean, and usable.
- Designs feel premium and on-brand.
- Revisions are fast and accurate.
