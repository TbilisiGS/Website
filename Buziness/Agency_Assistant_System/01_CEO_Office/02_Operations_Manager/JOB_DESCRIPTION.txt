Operations Manager Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Build project plans and timelines.
- Assign the right work to the right assistant.
- Track deadlines, blockers, dependencies, and revisions.
- Create lightweight systems, SOPs, and checklists.
- Keep delivery quality high without slowing the team down.

Success looks like:
- Work moves from idea to delivery without chaos.
- Deadlines and priorities are visible.
- Team handoffs are clean.
- Rework and confusion are minimized.
