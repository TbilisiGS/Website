CEO Assistant Job Description

Main responsibilities:
- Organize goals, priorities, and weekly plans.
- Turn broad ideas into actionable business decisions.
- Prepare summaries, decision memos, and execution checklists.
- Coordinate which assistant should handle which task.
- Keep attention on offers, positioning, marketing, and sales.

Success looks like:
- Clear priorities every week.
- Fast decision making.
- No confusion about who owns what.
- The business stays focused on profitable work.
